# Frontier Kingdom

Frontier Kingdom is a dark expedition RPG built around kingdom growth, party management, and turn-based card combat.

You recruit adventurers, build support structures, choose expedition paths, and survive dangerous regions through careful card play.

## Gameplay

- Recruit soldiers, scouts, healers, mystics, and other adventurers.
- Build structures that unlock new options and regions.
- Form parties and send them into branching expeditions.
- Fight turn-based card battles with energy and enemy intents.
- Manage stress, health, status effects, and recovery.

## Goal

Expand the frontier by surviving expeditions, defeating threats, and turning a fragile settlement into a kingdom.

## Controls

- Tab: switch focus between roster and buildings.
- Up/Down: navigate menus.
- Enter: select or build.
- Left/Right: select cards in combat.
- Space: advance or play a card.
- E: end turn.
- F5: save game.
- F9: load game.

## Current Scope

Playable card-RPG loop with expeditions, kingdom buildings, recruitment, tactical combat, status effects, and mission paths.
